Calendar

Ionic 2 Calendar (alpha)

A responsive calendar directive for Ionic 2 apps. Calendar items are draggable and can have custom event listeners for tap, doubletap and press events (test them on Item 2). Events are also fired when items are dragged to a new date. For more info check out the Github repository link below.

To create new events press the at the top of the page or hold down on an a cell in the calendar grid.
